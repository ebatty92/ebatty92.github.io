Welcome to The Cave

My name is Evan Batty, and this site serves as an online portfolio for my various projects related to sociology, cartography, and health.

Greenhouse Gas Emitters and Cancer-related Deaths in the U.S.

This map takes an environmental health approach to visualizing a correlation between greenhouse gas emissions and cancer-related deaths across the U.S. Additionally, it provides information on specific GHG emitters across the country.

Wildfires in the U.S.

Wildfires are an increasingly prevelant threat within the context of both climate change and residential expansion into heavily forested areas. This map shows which counties in the U.S., during the year of 2013, had the highest amount of cumulative area burned from wildfires, and zooming in on the map provides a closer look at individual fires.

Cancer Rates Across the U.S.

Cancer is the second leading cause of death globally. This map provides a visual representation of cancer-related deaths across the U.S. The trend of cancer deaths over the past 5 years is also provided for each county.

Medication Assisted Treatment Facilities Across Kentucky

Kentucky is one of the states hit hardest by the current opioid crisis in the U.S., and medication-assisted treatment is an important source of treatment. This map shows the density of MAT facilities across the state, highlighting the disparity in availability that exists in Appalachian counties.

About me

I'm a Sociologist and researcher specializing in web mapping and statistical analyses.

My research interests lie in Medical Sociology with a focus in drug misuse and stigma. My current research looks at how the knowledge and beliefs of healthcare professionals, regarding drug misuse and addiction, influence patient care in hospital emergency department settings.
